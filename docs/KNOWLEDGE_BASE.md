## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is XYZ Salon?**
A: XYZ Salon is a full-service beauty salon offering hair, nail, and skin services. Our experienced team provides professional services in a relaxing atmosphere.

**Q: Do I need an appointment?**
A: Appointments are recommended to guarantee your preferred time and stylist. However, we welcome walk-ins based on availability.

**Q: What should I bring to my appointment?**
A: Just yourself! We provide everything needed for your service. If you have hair inspiration photos, feel free to bring them for your consultation.

**Q: Is parking available?**
A: Yes, we have ample free parking available for all clients.

### Appointment Questions

**Q: How do I book an appointment?**
A: You can book online at xyzsalon.com, call us directly, or walk in for same-day availability.

**Q: Can I request a specific stylist?**
A: Absolutely! When booking, you can request your preferred stylist or select "first available."

**Q: What is your cancellation policy?**
A: We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may result in a fee.

**Q: What if I'm running late?**
A: Please call us as soon as possible. Late arrivals may result in shortened service time to accommodate other clients.

### Service Questions

**Q: How long does a typical appointment take?**
A: Service times vary - a simple haircut takes 30-45 minutes, while color services can take 2-3 hours. We'll let you know the expected time when you book.

**Q: Do you offer consultations?**
A: Yes! Every service includes a complimentary consultation to discuss your desired look and the best approach.

**Q: What products do you use?**
A: We use professional-grade products from leading brands to ensure the best results.

**Q: Can I purchase products to use at home?**
A: Yes! We carry professional products and our stylists can recommend the best products for your hair type and style.

### Pricing Questions

**Q: Do you offer any discounts?**
A: Yes! We offer a new client discount, referral discounts, birthday month specials, and membership benefits.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payments.

**Q: Is gratuity included?**
A: Gratuity is not included in service prices but is always appreciated for excellent service.
